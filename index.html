About



Recently I discovered the technique of tufting,
so my world is now full of rugs in all shapes,
colors and sizes. I also like to draw and paint
or experiment with photography.

In my work as an art and design teacher
I love to pass on my knowledge and experience
on how to see and cope with the world to others.

It's the interaction about art and design
that makes everything more interesting to me :)
